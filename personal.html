interests & beyond

// the research side of me

i'm genuinely fascinated by the question of how intelligent systems — biological or artificial — learn to see. the fact that humans can effortlessly recognize objects in cluttered scenes, while even the best neural networks can fail in systematic ways, feels like one of the most interesting open problems in science. most of my time goes here.

// sports & analytics

i co-founded Gaucho Sports Analytics because i believe sports is one of the most accessible entry points for learning data science. i love the intersection of statistics and competition — there's something satisfying about finding signal in something as chaotic as a basketball game.

// dance & culture

as president of UCSB Dhadkan, i spend a surprising amount of time thinking about event production, fundraising, and team management. running a 501(c)(3) non-profit while taking a full course load has taught me more about leadership than anything else. it's also just a lot of fun.

// day to day

you'll find me in a café with my laptop more often than not. i'm a coffee person, a firm believer that the best thinking happens in the middle of a long walk, and permanently interested in the question of how minds — human and machine — actually work.
